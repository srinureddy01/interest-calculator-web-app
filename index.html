About

Vaddi Lekka is a simple and easy-to-use tool that helps you calculate loan details effortlessly. Just enter the loan amount, interest rate, loan duration, start date, and end date. The tool will then provide you with the total loan duration, loan amount, applicable interest rate, principal amount, and the final amount to be paid. You can calculate both simple and compound interest quickly and accurately. Vaddi Lekka makes loan calculations easy and helps you plan your finances better.

Simple Interest Formula:

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

(Where P = Principal, R = Rate of Interest, T = Time in Years)

Compound Interest Formula:

CI = P (1 + R/100)^T - P
